About Us

Building Brighter Futures, One Child at a Time

NayePankh Foundation was born from a simple belief: that potential knows no address. We work in underserved communities across India, bridging the gap between aspiration and opportunity through education, mentorship, and skill-building.

📚 Education First 🌱 Grassroots Impact 🤝 Community-Led 💡 Skill Development

Our Mission

To provide accessible education and skill development to marginalized children and youth, enabling them to build self-reliant lives.

Our Vision

A society where every child — regardless of background — has the opportunity to dream, grow, and contribute meaningfully.

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Three pillars that shape our work — designed to create lasting change from the inside out.

📖

Education Support

From remedial coaching to digital literacy, we make quality education accessible for children who fall through the cracks of the formal system. After-school centres, scholarship support, and learning materials — all free.

Explore program →
🛠️

Skill Development

Vocational training programs for youth (16–25) covering tailoring, digital skills, spoken English, and sustainable livelihoods. We partner with local businesses to provide real placement support.

Explore program →
🏘️

Community Welfare

Health camps, women's empowerment circles, sanitation drives, and community kitchens. We address the whole ecosystem — because a child thrives when their community thrives too.
